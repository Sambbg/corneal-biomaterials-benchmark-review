# Pilot Screening Round 1 Audit

## Purpose

The first pilot screening round was completed using AI-assisted include/exclude decisions. Because AI screening can be overconfident, the decisions were manually audited before proceeding to formal PubMed export.

## Main Audit Finding

The original screening was broadly usable, but too confident. The most important concern was that it produced zero "Uncertain" decisions, which is unlikely in realistic title/abstract screening.

## Audit Correction

Several borderline records were changed from Include to Uncertain because they appeared biologically or clinically relevant but were not clearly core biomaterial, scaffold, fabrication, engineered substitute, or corneal repair/replacement evidence from title/abstract alone.

## Updated Decision Rule

Use:

- Include: directly usable for biomaterial/scaffold/cell-engineered corneal repair or replacement.
- Uncertain: corneal biology, wound repair, hydrogel/drug delivery, testing model, or ocular-surface study that may support the review but may not belong in the core extraction table.
- Exclude: review, book chapter, general disease, clinical surgery without engineering, unrelated ophthalmology.
