## Corrected Threshold

Include only when the title/abstract clearly indicates biomaterial, scaffold, engineered construct, cell sheet, decellularized matrix, tissue engineering, or corneal regeneration/reconstruction relevance.

Exclude routine clinical ophthalmology, keratoplasty, DMEK/DSAEK/DALK, imaging, outcomes, and surgical complication papers unless they clearly involve tissue engineering or biomaterial development.

Use Uncertain for biomechanics, collagen organization, wound healing, and model papers that may support benchmarking but are not clearly biomaterial/construct studies.
